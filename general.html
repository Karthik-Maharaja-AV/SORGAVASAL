GREEN ZONES

Green zones should be crime-free. No crime should be initiated from the green zone. It is Fail RP to run away from a crime and enter the green zone to avoid RP but police station is excluded from this rule. If the RP begins outside of it and is brought into the green zone, you may continue the criminal RP, however, it must be realistic. An example, in which it is not allowed is bringing a gang vs gang fight to the Hospital or Police station. Hospital, Premium Dealership, Police Department (PD), all Business areas. community service location is not a safe zone but initiating a RP with a person doing community service or a person released from jail is considered as a fail RP.
