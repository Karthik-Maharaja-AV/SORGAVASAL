GANG RULES

  • Avoid Toxicity in gang RP.
  • You should give proper respect to opposite gang members.
  • Try to adapt yourself to the Gang RP or else don’t participate in Gang RP.
  • Toxic words will be acceptable only in IC perspective. If the opposite gang people doesn’t like toxic words and they informed, you cannot continue using toxic words. It is your Duty to inform the opposition if you don’t like toxic words.
  • Personal Life or Personality abuse will not be tolerated.
  • Gang Leader should lead their gang properly. Gang leader is completely responsible for any mistakes made by the gang. (BOTH IC AND OOC).
  • Gang-leader must have basic RP knowledge. Gang leader will be questioned for any mistakes made by the gang.
  • You should not go into your gang-place after completing a robbery, use hideouts instead.
  • If any gang doesn't respond to opposite gang's takeover request in discord or in IC, the place will automatically goes to requested gang.
  • In your opposition’s gang place , The number of members will not be as equal to Yours (The whole Gang could be there). So it's necessary for a gang to show fear RP in their opponent’s gang place.
  • Don't link your Gang problem issue into Whitelisted Job like Mechanic,Customs and etc.., (Example “Gang A Not Selling Car for Gang B” should not do it.)
  • Alliance is allowed only when city achived at 5 official gangs. Team is not allowed between gang.
  • You may not advertise your gang through DMs or other public chats
